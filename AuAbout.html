What we're about Exploring world

BTC-B-Travels Club is also Known As Battu Travels Company.. BTC is to promote outdoor adventure activities and trekking in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. BTC is here just to connect people to the wonderful nature, which many of us have not experienced yet and also those who want to explore more. When we connect to nature, we explore picturesque mountains, places of natural beauty, making the environment beautiful and many other things. Having all this in life, provides refreshment to our soul and body.
